# BSPrep Ambassador Bounties (50-Day Program)

This document contains a curated list of high-value tasks (Bounties) designed to aggressively grow the brand while keeping ambassadors highly engaged over the 50-day marathon.

## 1. The "Guest Author" Bounty (Content Creation)
* **The Task:** Write a high-quality, 800+ word article about your exam preparation journey, a specific subject you mastered, or study hacks using BSPrep.
* **The Process:** Submit the draft via the dashboard. If approved by the team, it gets published on the official BSPrep Blog with your name as the Author.
* **Reward:** 300 Points

## 2. The "Meme Lord" Bounty (Viral Marketing)
* **The Task:** Create 3 highly relatable, funny memes about the struggles of studying for these exams, subtly featuring BSPrep as the solution.
* **The Process:** Submit them via the dashboard. The funniest ones get posted on the official BSPrep Instagram/Twitter, and the creator gets tagged.
* **Reward:** 100 Points (If selected for official posting)

## 3. The "Campus Workshop" Bounty (Mass Conversion)
* **The Task:** Organize and host a 30-minute offline session in an empty classroom, or a Google Meet session for your college peers, teaching them exactly how to use BSPrep to crack their exams.
* **The Process:** Submit a photo/screenshot showing at least 15 students attending the session.
* **Reward:** 500 Points

## 4. The "Feature Architect" Bounty (Deep Feedback)
* **The Task:** Record a 3-minute Loom video showing your screen. Walk through the BSPrep platform, review it, and pitch ONE major new feature you think we should build, explaining exactly why students need it.
* **The Process:** Submit the Loom video URL.
* **Reward:** 150 Points

## 5. The "Landing Page Star" Bounty (Testimonials)
* **The Task:** Record a professional, well-lit, 60-second video testimonial talking about your before-and-after experience with BSPrep.
* **The Process:** Upload the video. Only the top videos get selected to be permanently embedded on our main Landing Page.
* **Reward:** 400 Points (If selected)

## 6. The "Daily Streak" (Engagement)
* **The Task:** Log into your Ambassador Dashboard every single day.
* **The Process:** Automated. 
* **Reward:** +2 Points every day you log in. Maintain a 7-day streak for a +20 Point bonus.
